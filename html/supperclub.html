The Supper Club

You should have been at the last one.

The Wine Twit Supper Club: a four-course dinner, wines paired to every course, and the sort of evening that turns into a late one.

Twice a month, we close the shop, push the tables together, and chef Lawrence does what he does best. Every menu is built around the wines, not bolted on afterwards. Which means the food and the bottles talk to each other in a way they rarely do at even the best restaurants. Twenty covers. Candles. A room full of people who showed up because they love good food and good wine. We've been running these for years and we still look forward to every single one.

Supper Club Details

  • 4-course menu with paired wines throughout
  • Resident chef: Lawrence [surname]
  • 20 covers per sitting — deliberately small
  • £85 per person, all wine included
  • Nightingale Lane, Clapham SW12
  • Twice monthly — dates listed below
Delicious three-course dinners with wine paired to each course by The Wine Twit himself. Each event has a different theme, menu and wine on offer. You’ll never want to cook for yourself again!

     
Who doesn’t love eating out? No dishes to wash, no table to clear, no arguments over whose turn it is to cook. But, even better than just eating out is discovering something special before everyone else. A place where you can indulge in great food and phenomenal wine, paired to perfection. A place that only those in the know have uncovered, where the tables are full and the atmosphere is light. A place to learn more about wine, catch up with friends – old and new – and maybe even go on a date or two.
